Announcing the 2013 BHN Gaming LAN!

On 20 October, 2013 at noon we will be hosting our 5th free to attend LAN party in the Tampa Bay area, and 3rd for the public (ending at 0200 on the 21st of October, 2013). Brought to you by the efforts and contributions from Bright House Networks, CDW, Dimension Printing, Kontrol Freek, Microsoft, Netgear, Performance Computer Group, Plantronics Gamescom, PNPLAN, Red Bull, and Velocity Micro! So go ahead and pack up your computer and or console and come enjoy hours of gaming in a social environment! We hope to see you there!
